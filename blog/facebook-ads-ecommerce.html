10 Facebook Ad Copy Templates for E-Commerce (Steal These Today)

15 min readMay 7, 2026

Facebook advertising is still the #1 paid acquisition channel for e-commerce brands. But here's the problem — most sellers write terrible ad copy that burns money.

We've analyzed thousands of high-performing Facebook ads from 7-figure Shopify stores and distilled them into 10 copy-paste templates you can use today.

Template 1: The PAS (Problem-Agitation-Solution)

The PAS framework is the most reliable ad structure in existence. It works because it mirrors how humans process decisions: recognize a problem → feel the pain → seek relief.

📋 Template
Headline: "Still struggling with [PAIN POINT]?"
"You've tried [OLD SOLUTION]. You've wasted money on [COMPETITOR]. And you're STILL dealing with [PROBLEM]. Here's the truth: [PROBLEM] won't fix itself. But [PRODUCT] was designed specifically for people like you who are tired of [AGITATION]. In just [TIMEFRAME], you'll [TRANSFORMATION]. ⭐ Rated 4.9/5 by [NUMBER]+ customers."
CTA: Shop Now / Get Yours Today

Template 2: The Social Proof Review

Ads that look like customer reviews outperform branded ads by 4x because they feel authentic and trustworthy.

📋 Template
Headline: "⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ I can't believe I waited this long"
"I was skeptical at first, but [PRODUCT] completely changed my [ROUTINE/LIFE]. The [FEATURE] is incredible — I've never experienced anything like it. My [FRIEND/PARTNER] noticed the difference in just [TIMEFRAME]. I've already ordered 2 more as gifts. If you're on the fence, just DO IT. You won't regret it."
CTA: See Why 10,000+ People Love It

Template 3: The FOMO / Urgency Play

Scarcity and urgency are the most powerful conversion triggers in advertising. Use them ethically but aggressively.

📋 Template
Headline: "🚨 [X]% OFF — Ends Tonight"
"We're running our BIGGEST sale of the year on [PRODUCT] — but only for the next [TIMEFRAME]. [NUMBER] people are viewing this right now. Last time we did this, we sold out in [TIMEFRAME]. Don't be the person who sees this tomorrow and kicks themselves."
CTA: Claim Your Discount Before Midnight

Template 4: The Before/After Transformation

📋 Template
Headline: "[TIME] ago I was [BEFORE STATE]. Now I'm [AFTER STATE]."
"Before [PRODUCT]: [PAIN]. After [PRODUCT]: [TRANSFORMATION]. The difference? Just [ONE SIMPLE CHANGE]. See the results for yourself 👇"
CTA: Start Your Transformation

Template 5: The Contrarian Hook

📋 Template
Headline: "Stop doing [COMMON ADVICE]. Here's what actually works."
"Everyone tells you to [COMMON ADVICE] to fix [PROBLEM]. But after working with [NUMBER]+ customers, we discovered that [CONTRARIAN INSIGHT]. That's why we built [PRODUCT] differently..."
CTA: Learn the Better Way
