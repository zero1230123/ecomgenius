Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Welcome to EcomGenius and the PagePilot AI Chrome Extension. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

EcomGenius SaaS: When you register, we collect your email address and basic profile information via Google or standard email sign-up. We also securely store your API keys locally on your device; they are never transmitted to our servers.

PagePilot AI Chrome Extension: The extension requires specific permissions to function:

  • activeTab & <all_urls>: Used strictly to read the content, meta tags, and visual styles of the active webpage you are analyzing. We do not track your browsing history.
  • storage: Used to save your settings and license key locally on your device.
  • scripting: Used to inject the analyzer tool into the active tab to extract SEO and performance data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to provide and improve our services. For the Chrome Extension, page content is only analyzed locally or sent directly to the AI provider (OpenRouter/OpenAI/Anthropic) using your provided API key to generate insights. We do not store or sell your analyzed webpage data.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Firebase: For secure user authentication.
  • PayPal & Gumroad: For processing secure payments. We do not store your credit card information.
  • OpenRouter: For processing AI requests.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. API keys are stored locally in your browser's local storage or extension storage and are only used for direct communication with the AI providers.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@ecomgenius.app.
